Meet Sabiha

Certified Nail Artist & Nail Care Expert

Sabiha is a highly skilled nail artist with a diploma in Nail Art and Design. Her passion for creativity and perfection is reflected in every design she creates. Specializing in nail extensions, custom nail art, and elegant manicures, she ensures that every client leaves with beautifully crafted nails.

Beyond just nail art, Sabiha is dedicated to the overall health and well-being of nails. She educates her clients on proper nail care, helping them maintain strong, healthy nails underneath the art.

Originally from Mumbai, Sabiha holds a postgraduate degree in Human Resources. Her love for nail artistry led her to pursue her professional diploma. She now blends creativity with care, helping clients express their style and celebrate nail health.
